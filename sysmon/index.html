7/13 - G30-135 Don't Use Alarms

The G30-135 alarmed to Don't Use at 2022-07-13 18:56:59. This time corresponds to a maneuver which the Operators uploaded earlier in the day at 15:33:18. 7/12 - DX1 Scheduled Maintenance

DXR SGS scheduled for intrusive CM on L-Band receive path/ antenna PM; DX1 GUS Switchover to Backup at 7/12 07:00. DX1 Maintenance started at 7/12 13:00; returned to Backup Mode 7/12 20:02.

7/12-7/13 - MPR WRE-B Processor Replaced / Rebaselined

The MPR WRE-B processor was rebaselined and installed this week on 7/12-7/13. During the initial installation attempt on 7/12, the processor was inadvertently powered on with a dead battery causing the O&Ms to crash at 7/12 05:40. The STR guidance around the dead time-of-day batteries has been approved and is pending release, FYI.

Additionally, after MPR WRE-B was successfully installed and rebaselined on 7/13, we discovered an issue with the WRE-B Frequency Standard which requires replacement.
