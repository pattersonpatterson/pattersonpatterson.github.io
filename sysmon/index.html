Highlights

7/27 - SZ1 Down for Maintenance

Raytheon request a 2 hour maintenance window on 7/27 at 2100Z for Santa Paula/SZ1 to do follow up measurements on the KPA units. Returned to Backup at 7/27 23:35.

7/12-7/29 - MPR WRE-B Restored to Normal

After MPR WRE-B was successfully installed and rebaselined on 7/13, we discovered an issue with the WRE-B Frequency Standard. The Frequency Standard was replaced, but the new Frequency Standard will not initialize; cycling through Maintenance Mode. Tech to check configuration.

Spare Freq Std could not be configured, and MPR is working with the MX in-country store to get a new spare Freq Std. The original Freq Std was re-installed, and successfully initialized.

MPR WRE-B was restored to Normal at 7/29 14:09. Currently monitoring operation.

7/29 - BET WRE-C Freq Std Minor Alarm

BET WRE-C was taken offline to address Freq Std Minor Alarm. WAAS Second level and WAAS Operators discussed being more lenient with non-critical faults: we should not pre-emptively take a functional subsystem offline to address a non-critical fault. Subsystems should only be removed from service via fault or following coordination with WAAS Engineers and/or site technicians.

7/31 - CM1 GUS Faulted from Backup

CM1 GUS faulted from Backup with a KPA arc fault. CM1 is currently using the standby KPA. Raytheon is planning to replace the bad KPA in the near future.

8/3 - YYR WRE-A Restored to Normal

YYR site technicians received the Freq Std cable and replaced the part, but the Freq Std data was not affected. YYR WRE-A is currently in Normal Mode being monitored, but the thread status for YYR WRE-A is BAD.

8/3 - AP1 Ring 1 PRI / Ring 2 ALT Circuits Looped

During FTI MR 160206A, the AP1 circuits became looped. This Troubleshooting is ongoing.
