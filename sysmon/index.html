Highlights

YYR WRE-A Monitor in Normal

YYR WRE-A is currently in Normal Mode being monitored, but the thread status for YYR WRE-A is inconsistent. Site technicians performed fault isolation tests on 8/5. The phase noise is still elevated on all bands, and it is recommended to replace the frequency standard again.

8/3-8/5 - AP1 Ring 1 PRI / Ring 2 ALT Circuits Looped

During FTI MR 160206A, the AP1 circuits became looped. This Troubleshooting is ongoing. Troubleshooting included GUS Switchover and full resets of the ZLA COR-A and ZTL COR-B routers. Issue cleared on 8/5.

8/4 - BET WRE-C Freq Std Issues

BET WRE-C was taken offline to troubleshoot Frequency Standard related faults. The site technician replaced the Freq Std with the spare, but the spare seems to be in even worse condition. The site tech restored the original Freq Std, and is looking to return the bad spare. No issues since the original Freq Std was restored.

OTZ WRS High Temperatures

OTZ WRS has experienced fluctuating high temperatures since 8/5. On 8/7, the high temperatures caused the WRE-C AGC gain to spike on all bands. On 8/9, the Operators performed a protective shutdown on all WREs at the OTZ WRS. Temperatures still running high.
