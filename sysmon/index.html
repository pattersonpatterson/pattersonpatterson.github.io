Highlights

CM1 KPA Replaced

8/19 - CM1 replaced the bad KPA from 7/31 fault.

YYR WRE-A Monitor in Maintenance

On 8/16 YYR WRE-A was set to Maintenance to avoid further unplanned WRS outages due to WRE Bias. Site technicians performed fault isolation tests on 8/5 and found no apparent issues. The phase noise is still elevated on all bands, and it is recommended to replace the frequency standard again.

Site is waiting for parts.

YYR WRE-C PCU and Freq Std Issues

8/18 02:45 - YYR WRE-C faulted from Normal with Freq Std faults. Since then, the Freq Std has been running with alarm code 244 (Ion Pump Overcurrent) indicating end of life. Additionally, The PCU-C has been running with Alarm condition 0, which did not clear after a power cycle of the PCU. It is recommended to replace PCU-C and then replace the frequency standard.

Site is waiting for parts.

OTZ WRS High Temperatures -- in Normal

OTZ WRS has experienced fluctuating high temperatures since 8/5. On 8/7, the high temperatures caused the WRE-C AGC gain to spike on all bands. Operators performed a few protective shutdowns to prevent damage to equipment due to high temps. OTZ technicians set up a vent fan on 8/19 to keep the temperatures below 40 degrees Celsius.

OTZ HVAC needs repaired, but OTZ can remain in Normal Mode.

CDB Ring 2 Down Hard since 8/6

So far, FTI circuit from ZAN to ZAU tests good, and ASTI satcoms are apparently testing good too. A site technician should be onsite to troubleshoot around 8/24
